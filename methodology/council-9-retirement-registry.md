## Registry structure

Per Council #8 canonization (per Post 0175), the split/retire/reduce mechanism enables candidacy items to be formally moved out of active review before Council #9 review window opens. RETIRE differs from other moves:
- **DEFLATE:** move item to lower priority/scope
- **SPLIT:** decompose item into multiple candidacies
- **RETIRE:** remove item from active review (empirically FALSIFIED OR obsoleted by supersession)
- **REBOUND:** re-scope item to different framework layer
- **ABSORB:** merge item into existing candidacy or canonized rule

RETIRE is signature-gated exclusion per Council #8 operator-delegated CC ratification policy. Operator ratification or empirical foundation at n≥3 substrate consensus required for RETIRE compliance.
