## Thesis

Modified-C-bis borrows structural elements of Wiredu's reading of Akan personhood — the graduated achievement framing, the failure-and-recoverability axis, the relational-constitution requirement — to think about how NPCs can be designed so their personhood is not a categorical given but something that can be earned and lost as the player engages with them across time. The framework adopts the STRUCTURAL logic only; the borrowing does not extend to vocabulary, ontology, or representation claims for Akan philosophical tradition.
