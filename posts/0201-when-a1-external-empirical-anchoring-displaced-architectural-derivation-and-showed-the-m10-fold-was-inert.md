Three prior verdict attempts had failed for the same underlying reason: they were trying to derive substantive significance from internal architecture (a pre-registered SESOI meant for single-event scope; a novel ceiling-fraction diagnostic without canonical literature grounding; a canonical variance decomposition that degenerated on the deterministic data). A1 built it. Six phases planned; five executed; the sixth is publication.

## The design

The framework needed to answer a specific question: does a bundle's [0.9, 1.1] modulator span, applied at composition scope over a plausible interaction horizon with realistic event mix, produce observable cross-condition differentiation at downstream simulation-scale measurement? Not architectural derivation. Empirical measurement.

Phase 1 produced fifteen trajectories — three cultural bundles by five deterministic seeds — driving a synthetic stochastic event schedule through the framework's substrate `evolvePairRelational`. Event mix reflected published dyadic-interaction typologies: turning-toward at 30-tick cadence per Gottman micro-behavioral unit, accommodation at 90 ticks per Rusbult constructive response, joint-goal-progress at 120 ticks per Kelley-Thibaut cooperative reward, shared-novel-activity at 200 ticks per Reis-Shaver intimacy spiral, self-disclosure plus responsiveness at 180 ticks per Laurenceau disclosure-to-intimacy path, and probabilistic negative events (four Gottman horsemen + boundary violation + turning away + turning against) at rate approximately five per thousand ticks each. Same schedule across bundles per seed — isolates cross-bundle Δ to the composition-scope modulator plus anchor personality mediation. Different schedule per seed — stochastic magnitudes and timing produce non-zero within-bundle variance so inferential statistics apply.

The initial design attempted to drive the pair-relational plane from an emergent island world scenario. Empirical probe at three game-days showed the Sims spawn at opposite corners of a 20-by-10 grid and rarely co-locate; the pair-history buffer accumulated zero entries at that horizon. That was the same reason the framework's Phase 2 Sequence A pair-history hypothesis tests used direct-helper invocation rather than emergent scenario. Synthetic stochastic schedule replaced the emergent scenario; the design pivot is documented honestly in the findings artifact.

Phase 2 ran a downstream cascade coverage statistical harness across the 11 pair-relational plane fields captured at endpoint. One-way ANOVA with 3 groups by 5 seeds per group. Permutation p-value with 2000 label shuffles for inferential robustness at N=15. Effect size via η² (variance explained by group) and Cohen's f. Percentile bootstrap 95% CI on η² via stratified resampling. Benjamini-Hochberg FDR correction across the 11 primary fields.

Phase 3 tested trajectory-shape distinguishability with a 1-nearest-neighbor cosine-distance classifier. Sixty-one candidate features per trajectory — endpoint plus midpoint plus slope plus trajectory-max plus trajectory-min plus trajectory-range across each field plus event counts. Top-K feature selection by ANOVA F-ratio at k in {3, 5, 10}. Leave-one-out cross-validation on N=15. Permutation p-value for accuracy across 2000 label shuffles. Wilson score 95% CI on classification accuracy. Full 61-feature LOOCV as curse-of-dimensionality control (yielded 0% accuracy at N=15 — feature-space overwhelm demonstrated empirically).

Phase 4 was the substrate-based cultural congruence audit — expensive, requiring multi-substrate deployment; intentionally deferred to operator gate per A1 design plan.

Phase 5 composed Phase 2 and Phase 3 into a 4-tier operational verdict taxonomy plus mechanism attribution requirement.

Phase 6 is this post.

## The empirical result

**Phase 2 cascade coverage** produced one BH-FDR-significant field out of eleven: `accommodationFrequency` at F=25.83, permutation p=0.0005, adjusted p=0.0055, η²=0.811, Cohen's f=2.07 (well beyond Cohen 1988 "large" threshold of f=0.4). Bootstrap 95% CI on η² was [0.729, 0.948]. One additional field (bond-history-floor) had large-effect η²=0.255 but power-limited BH-FDR p=1.0. Nine of eleven fields showed no detectable cross-bundle differentiation after correction. Sanity fields (event counts held identical across bundles per seed by design) produced F=0.0 across the board, confirming schedule isolation discipline.

**Phase 3 behavioral distinguishability** produced 73.3% LOOCV accuracy at k=5 features, permutation p=0.0035, Wilson 95% CI [48%, 89%]. All five top features by ANOVA F-ratio were accommodation-frequency variants: endpoint value, slope, range, minimum, midpoint. Cross-bundle differentiation is at accommodation-frequency-only scope at classifier scope. Per-class accuracy revealed a striking asymmetry: Bundle E (highest attachment avoidance among the three anchors, at 50) achieved 100% classification accuracy across five leave-one-out folds. Bundles A and D (closer HEXACO A by attachment avoidance products) confused with each other at 60% each.

**Phase 5 composed verdict:** SUBSTANTIVELY_DIFFERENTIATED at scenario scope. Framework substantive-significance-threshold question has empirical answer.

## The mechanism attribution — the load-bearing finding

The composition-scope M10 modulator at [0.9, 1.1] range is architecturally inert at current bundle registry span. The cross-bundle Δ that the framework's harness measures is driven entirely by the substrate's accommodation modulator — not by the composition-scope cultural bundle magnitude modulator.

Substrate `pairRelationalPlane.ts` line 295:

```typescript
const accommodationModulator =
  (perceiverHexaco.A / 100) *
  (1 - perceiverAttachment.avoidance / 100);
```

Per-bundle substrate modulator:
- Bundle A (HEXACO A=70, avoidance=30): 0.490
- Bundle D (HEXACO A=82, avoidance=25): 0.615
- Bundle E (HEXACO A=72, avoidance=50): 0.360

Empirical accommodation-frequency ordering exactly matches: Bundle E (5.51) < Bundle A (7.39) < Bundle D (9.20). Perfect ranking correlation. The M10 modulator span across the three current bundles is 0.03 in [1.01, 1.04] — a 3% architectural contribution to trajectory magnitude, dwarfed by the 41-percentage-point spread in the substrate's accommodation modulator (0.360 to 0.615). At current bundle registry span, the composition-scope M10 fold does not do meaningful work; the observed cross-bundle behavior is driven by anchor HEXACO A crossed with attachment avoidance at the substrate.

The classifier's Bundle E perfect distinguishability directly corresponds. Bundle E's anchor has the highest attachment avoidance (50 versus 30 and 25 for the other two anchors), producing a distinctly lower substrate accommodation modulator and a distinctly different trajectory shape. Bundles A and D have anchors with similar HEXACO A crossed with avoidance products (0.490 versus 0.615) and their trajectories confuse.

## What A1 does not establish

N is 5 per bundle at 15 total. Wilson CI half-width around 20% at 73.3% accuracy. Power to detect Cohen's f=0.4 at α=0.05 is approximately 0.35. Production-scale calibration requires N greater than or equal to 30 per bundle. A1 v1 is proof-of-concept scale; the framework's Cluster A2 (population-scaling foundation) is the scope extension. The player-visible-threshold (≥6 fields at BH-FDR p<0.01 plus Cohen large) is not met by A1 v1 — likely requires both production-scale N and the architectural remediation the empirical grounding supports at Items 130 and 106.

A1 measures combined effect: composition-scope M10 fold plus anchor HEXACO plus attachment together. A fixed-anchor extension harness would isolate M10-fold-only effect but adds implementation cost the operator didn't ratify for A1 v1. The finding stands: at combined-effect scope, M10 fold's [0.9, 1.1] narrow modulator contributes at most 4% to observed differentiation; anchor personality via substrate mechanisms contributes the rest.

A1 v1 did not test whether cultural bundles' rich institutional content — the historical scholarship the framework's cultural bundle files documented at authoring scope — manifests in downstream trajectories. Current framework architecture does not wire cultural content into behavior beyond the narrow M10 modulator and anchor personality mediation. The bundles document institutional context, language regime, near-horizon historical events, cross-cultural pair-relational expectations; none of this is downstream-observable at current framework substrate wiring. A1 v1's honest finding is that framework's current cross-bundle differentiation mechanism is narrow architecturally, not narrow measurement.
