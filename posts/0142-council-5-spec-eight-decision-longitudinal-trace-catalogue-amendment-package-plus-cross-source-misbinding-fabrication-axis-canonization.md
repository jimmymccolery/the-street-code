## Decision 2: Longitudinal-benchmark methodology guide creation

Council #5 close creates a new methodology guide at `~/Projects/the-street-code/methodology/longitudinal-benchmark-methodology-guide-v1.md` parallel in shape to Post 0130 relationship-mode ablation gate guide plus Post 0135 per-NPC-philosophy-profile reservation-and-ablation-gate guide plus Post 0140 appraisal-tag reservation-and-ablation-gate guide. The methodology guide documents Substrate D's four answer-key shapes plus Substrate B's endpoint-plus-intermediate pass criterion plus Substrate B's directional ablation criteria plus Substrate D's six-condition reversal branch verbatim plus Substrate B's 24 to 44 operator-attention-hour Tier 1 cost estimate.

**The four answer-key shapes per Substrate D:**

**Invariant keys**: given event X was encoded as landmark at time T, later decision Y must have access to that landmark unless an explicit forgetting/compression rule applies. The invariant key shape is the direct operationalization of Post 0119's Markov-blanket isolation test at longitudinal cadence (audit-layer cadence extended to per-consolidation-boundary check).

**Metamorphic keys**: re-run the same trajectory with one variable changed (relationship asymmetry or cultural baseline or trait routing) and require a directionally different but internally coherent result. The metamorphic key shape operationalizes Post 0135 Rider 3 spec-gap-benchmark-independent structure at scenario-trace scope.

**Ablation keys**: disable a mechanism and require measurable degradation following the ablation-gate logic in Post 0130 for relationship-mode operationalization plus Post 0135 for per-NPC-philosophy-profile plus Post 0140 for appraisal-tag consolidation. The ablation key shape reuses the framework's canonical ablation-gate methodology at trace scope.

**Audit-alignment keys**: compare cached-policy behavior against the audit-layer evaluator and flag divergences where the cached policy violates the current accumulator state, matching Post 0119's audit-first design at longitudinal cadence.

**The endpoint-plus-intermediate pass criterion per Substrate B:** each trace requires endpoint action match plus at least one intermediate-state match (pair-summary sign/magnitude bucket, Layer 3 stress-valence direction, or cached-policy top-action rank). Endpoint-only tests are insufficient; the intermediate-state check catches regression cases where endpoint action happens to match under incorrect intermediate machinery.

**The directional ablation criterion per Substrate B:** ablation pass criteria are directional rather than overfit numeric. Disabling pair-summary consolidation should selectively harm relationship-dependent endpoints; disabling slow consolidation should selectively harm multi-year endpoints; disabling trait routing should flatten personality-specific response style; disabling H4 sparse-coding mechanism should expose compression edge cases per Post 0137; disabling H5 cultural-baseline mechanism should expose cultural-signal fabrication risks per Post 0138.

**The six-condition reversal branch per Substrate D verbatim** (demote to REQUIRES-SYNTHETIC-SCENARIOS if any fire):

1. Phase 2 two-Sim runs do not produce analyzable pair-history, asymmetry, or landmark-ripple sub-trajectories after a reasonable run budget.
2. Phase 2+ logs cannot reconstruct causal chains from event to memory write to consolidation to later decision.
3. Empirical trajectories show mostly happy-path routine behavior and under-sample rare/high-surprise, adversarial-history, or group-pressure cases.
4. Post-hoc answer keys prove unstable across seeds because the same labeled fire cannot be replayed or reduced to a deterministic harness case.
5. The first Phase 2+ review finds that the four-audit surface catches only structural violations while missing longitudinal consistency, social-goal, or action-level failures that benchmark-derived synthetic traces catch.
6. The X-Phi pilot needs specific player-facing vignettes that cannot be grounded in empirical Phase 2 trajectories quickly enough for the July-August 2026 pilot timeline described in Post 0114.

**The Tier 1 cost estimate per Substrate B:** 24 to 44 operator-attention hours composed of 4 to 8 hours for normalized fixture schema and runner plus 10 to 18 hours for 14 trace fixtures and endpoint assertions plus 5 to 9 hours for pair-summary plus Layer 3 plus cached-policy inspection outputs plus 3 to 6 hours for ablation toggles plus 2 to 3 hours for audit-report formatting. Comparable to relationship-mode ablation gate at approximately 25 to 48 hours per Post 0130 companion guide lines 360 through 370 plus per-NPC-philosophy per-family gate at approximately 21 to 42 hours per Post 0135 companion guide lines 355 through 366 but below appraisal-tag composed gate at approximately 33 to 64 hours per Post 0140 companion guide lines 192 through 203 if the catalogue remains internal and does not include playtester ratification. Cost anchors verified against the framework's published methodology-guide cost tables.
