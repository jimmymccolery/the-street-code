## The six fires

The pre-arc empirical session showed six mechanics not producing the behavior their specifications described. Sim A (River, survivalist archetype) and Sim B (Alex, chronic-burnout archetype) both pinned at valence_f floor. Storm approaching with 100-tick warning window: zero shelter-adjacent actions. Fires built once each and then died, never rebuilt. Kindness mechanic empirically inert despite sentiment reaching plus one. Twenty-two aggression events between a bonded pair. Nineteen signal-for-rescue attempts, zero rescues.

The six reshapes shipped sequentially with re-validation between iterations:

**Fire A** raised FAST_DECAY_RATE from 0.0008 to 0.003, reduced needs valence coupling from minus 0.5 to minus 0.2, and added a new computeBiographicalValenceBaseline helper that sums intensity-scaled formative event offsets. A per-tick drift toward that biographical baseline was added parallel to the existing HEXACO Emotionality stress drift inside applyHexacoStressDriftAndLonging. River shifted from valence_f mean minus 98.65 to plus 1.40. Alex from minus 99.05 to minus 16.78. Difference 18.18 points of biographical archetype differentiation persistent across the 30-day session.

**Fire B** required five iterations. The first four shipped increasingly-aggressive weight boosts on shelter-adjacent actions during stormApproaching: multiplicative 4x, then additive floor at 3.5, then 8.0, then post-emergency-modulation override. All four empirically produced zero point zero percent. Fifth iteration succeeded at 100.0 percent only after diagnostic instrumentation revealed the root cause: Sim A was mid-sleep through the entire storm-approach window. The sleep action started around tick 6700 with base duration 480 ticks. Storm-approach fired at tick 7100. Sim slept unbroken through 7101, 7121, 7141, 7161, 7181 as diagnostic logging showed. decideNextAction never ran, so the shelter-approach floor had no target to apply to. Fire B v5 added a storm-approach WAKE-UP INTERRUPT for sleep, sleep_in_shelter, rest, and observe_surroundings actions during the stormApproaching flag.

**Fire C** loosened the build_fire threshold from exposure at least 40 to exposure at least 20 OR no active fire on grid. Added a 2.5x rebuild boost when no fire exists (caveman-realistic: fire is central survival infrastructure). Raised tend_fire weight from 0.9 to 2.5. Result: 30-plus builds plus tending across the session. River fire skill jumped from 80 to 98.2.

**Fire D** loosened kindness synthesis gates on three axes. share_food gate widened from hasCaughtFish to hasCaughtFish OR foodCacheLevel at least 20 (also fixed a bug: the typo state.foodCache should have been state.foodCacheLevel; no test path had exercised the OR-branch). friendDistressed threshold lowered from valence_f minus 30 to minus 8 (post-Fire-A the biographical baseline range makes minus 30 empirically unreachable). COMFORT_STRIVING_WEIGHT raised from 0.8 to 2.5 (prior weight 0.8 times friendStressNormalized 0.1 to 0.3 yielded 0.08 to 0.24 which lost to all competing actions). River kindness skill reached 4.7 from prior zero.

**Fire E** required two iterations. Version one raised HOSTILE_SENTIMENT_THRESHOLD from minus 0.3 to minus 0.55 and AGGRESSION_OWN_CRISIS_THRESHOLD from 85 to 90. Aggression fires dropped from 22 to 3 but the cascade was not broken. Version two added AGGRESSION_COOLDOWN_TICKS at 1440 (one game-day), a new lastAggressionTick state field with perspective-swap propagation via simBView, and raised DESPERATION_OVERRIDE_THRESHOLD from 95 to 98. One aggression fire in the 30-day session. Sentiment stabilized at plus one point zero.

**Fire F** raised RESCUE_P_BASE from 0.05 to 0.08. At logistic saturation P equals 0.16 per signal at high_point no-storm rather than 0.10. Both Sims rescued at tick 30510, game-day 21.2.
