## Decision 1: Post 0128 schema-placeholder amendment adding appraisal-tags[] slot at Layer 2

Post 0128 in this arc canonized the framework's WEIRD-defaults-in-the-accumulator schema-placeholder discipline reserving per-character metadata fields (`cultural_context_tag` plus `intersectional_position_tags[]` plus `identity_stage`) as schema placeholders. Council #2's Decision 1 forward-reference amendment extends the schema-placeholder discipline to include a Layer-2-consolidation-cadence appraisal-tag slot.

The framework's forward-reference amendment (parallel to Post 0133 pattern; the framework's immutability convention prevents retro-editing prior posts; the correction lives in a new post carrying the amendment via forward reference) adds the following schema-placeholder slot to the framework's canonized per-character metadata surface.

**`appraisal_tags[]`** at Layer 2 consolidation cadence, with named nullable member fields. The slot is reserved at the schema surface at V1; the slot's members are consolidation-only computed (no per-frame appraisal computation) and applied at the game-year consolidation pass to Layer 1 events that promote to Layer 2 per the Post 0118 four promotion criteria (affective spike plus pair-history significance plus trait-modulated significance plus first-occurrence). The slot's member fields at Council #2 close:

**`agency_attribution`**: nullable enum encoding the source-of-cause structure of the promoted Layer 1 event. The enum takes values self-caused plus other-caused-intentional plus other-caused-accidental plus structural plus uncertain. The field is nullable because not every affective memory has an identifiable agent; a Layer 1 event whose cause is ambient environment or unresolved-causal-antecedent may carry `agency_attribution=null` at consolidation. The field is populated at consolidation cadence by the framework's event-generation pathway plus consolidation-time interpretation (specific implementation branch specified in the companion methodology guide `~/Projects/the-street-code/methodology/appraisal-tag-reservation-and-ablation-gate-v1.md`).

**`coping_control`**: nullable scalar encoding the perceived controllability of the promoted Layer 1 event's outcome or repair-affordance. The scalar takes values in the -1.0 to +1.0 range where -1.0 encodes total lack of control and +1.0 encodes full control-and-repair-affordance. The field is nullable because not every affective memory has a coping-relevance dimension; a Layer 1 event that is neither threat-relevant nor goal-relevant may carry `coping_control=null`. The field is populated at consolidation cadence per the companion methodology guide.

**`goal_congruence`**: nullable scalar as CANDIDATE-PENDING-GATE status per Decision 2. The scalar would take values in the -1.0 to +1.0 range where -1.0 encodes total goal-obstruction and +1.0 encodes total goal-congruence. The field's inclusion in the reserved-slot member list is contingent on the ablation-gate outcome per Decision 3; the framework reserves the field at Council #2 close pending gate ratification with explicit CANDIDATE-PENDING-GATE status disclosed in the schema-placeholder documentation.

**`expectedness`**: NOT reserved as a member field at V1 per Decision 2 plus Decision 5 (Substrate B's Condition-4 clamp-prediction-error-versus-H4 test). The framework's Post 0137 H4 prediction-error sparse encoding machinery may already carry the expectedness-signal at the mechanism-layer; a redundant Layer 2 appraisal-tag member field would waste consolidation-time processing without adding unique signal. The field's exclusion is pending Substrate B's ablation gate; if the gate reveals expectedness is not H4-duplicate at multi-Sim scope, the field may be added at subsequent Council methodology deployment.

**`norm_violation`**: NOT reserved as a universal Layer 2 member field at V1 per Decision 2 plus Substrate D's canonized-architecture grounding. Norm-violation routes through Post 0135 in this arc Decision 4 per-NPC-philosophy-profile normative dispatch behind the existing reservation-plus-ablation gate. The framework's continued architectural work on the per-NPC-philosophy-profile BANK (documented in Post 0135 Decision 4 spec plus the methodology guide `~/Projects/the-street-code/methodology/per-npc-philosophy-profile-reservation-and-ablation-gate-v1.md`) carries norm-violation as a profile-family-specific rule-set produced at consolidation cadence via the `profile_norm_breach_summary` field per the profile family's canonized normative rule-set. The universal Layer 2 appraisal-tag slot does NOT carry a `norm_violation=true` member because standard-relative appraisal requires per-NPC normative dispatch that only the per-NPC-philosophy-profile architecture provides.

**Reservation-plus-ablation adoption shape**: the slot plus member fields are reserved at V1-spec level now via this forward-reference amendment to Post 0128; the slot's operationalization (population-scale ratification that appraisal-tag signal survives partialling against sentiment scalar plus HEXACO-H plus attachment plus typed-index categorization at multi-Sim scope) is banked behind the appraisal-tag ablation gate specified in the companion methodology guide at `~/Projects/the-street-code/methodology/appraisal-tag-reservation-and-ablation-gate-v1.md` plus the per-NPC-philosophy-profile ablation gate Condition-2 modification per Decision 3.

## Decision 2: Reduced-set composition ratification at per-item Option B deliberation

Council #2's five substrates produced five different reduced-set proposals with substantive fold-set divergence on specific member fields. The orchestrator's Section 3 synthesized verdict applied primary-text adjudication (Smith and Ellsworth 1985 six orthogonal dimensions plus CiF-Ex bundled-intervention caveat plus framework-canon-verified Post 0118 four promotion criteria) to converge on the specific composition documented in Decision 1. **Composition ratified at core reservation now**: `agency_attribution` plus `coping_control` as separate nullable fields per the Smith and Ellsworth 1985 primary-text finding (six orthogonal dimensions including self-other-responsibility/control plus situational-control as separate axes rather than the merged Power dimension Substrate A had proposed via GRID reading). **Composition candidate-pending-gate**: `goal_congruence` reserved as CANDIDATE-PENDING-GATE. Three-of-five substrates included the field; two-of-five judged it redundant with valence plus sentiment carrying same signal. The redundancy question is empirically decidable via the ablation gate specified in Decision 3 (partialling regression measures whether goal-congruence contributes unique variance beyond sentiment plus valence baselines). The redundancy question is also operator-answerable via disclosure of the framework's valence-channel semantics: if the Phase-1-implemented valence channel is immediate-hedonic-only, goal-congruence at multi-decade-horizon carries unique signal Substrate A's own Claim-4 falsifier described; if the valence channel already carries goal-referenced multi-horizon evaluation, goal-congruence is redundant.

**Composition excluded at V1 pending ablation**: `expectedness` excluded per Substrate B's Condition-4 clamp-prediction-error-versus-H4 test. Post 0137 in this arc canonized the H4 sparse-coding machinery empirically validating that routine event streams produce less-than-10-percent encoding rate via prediction-error thresholding. The framework's mechanism-layer already carries expectedness-signal via prediction-error magnitude at Layer 1 encoding; a redundant Layer 2 appraisal-tag field would waste consolidation-time processing without adding unique signal. Substrate B's Condition 4 empirically tests the duplication claim: clamp prediction error to equal values and release; if expectedness tags add no downstream effect after H4 is active, expectedness is duplicate and remains excluded from V1. The exclusion holds pending the ablation gate.

**Composition routed to per-NPC-philosophy-profile dispatch**: `norm_violation` NOT reserved as a universal Layer 2 tag. Norm-violation is standard-relative and profile-native per OCC theory (agent-action emotions evaluate actions as praiseworthy or blameworthy relative to the perceiving character's standards, not merely as negative-valence events). A universal Layer 2 tag would flatten profile-specific norm-set differences into decorative labeling. Post 0135 Decision 4 per-NPC-philosophy-profile architecture already reserves the `normative_profile` slot; each per-NPC-philosophy profile carries its own norm-set that dispatches at consolidation cadence via `profile_norm_breach_summary` field per Substrate D's specification. The norm-violation-carrier question routes through the per-NPC-philosophy ablation gate per Decision 3.

The reduced-set composition is genuinely contested at per-item level rather than convergent at composed-question level. Substrate A had `goal_congruence` as drop plus `expectedness` (Novelty) as keep. Substrate A' had `goal_congruence` as keep mandatory plus `expectedness` as conditional. Substrate B had `goal_congruence` as drop-covered-by-valence-and-EFE plus `expectedness` as drop-duplicate-with-H4. Substrate C had `goal_congruence` as keep plus `expectedness` as banked-V1.x. Substrate D had `goal_congruence` as keep plus `expectedness` as keep. The framework's Option B per-item deliberation acknowledges the substantive divergence and adopts the orchestrator's primary-text-adjudicated composition with per-item fold-status disclosure (CORE plus CANDIDATE-PENDING-GATE plus EXCLUDED-PENDING-ABLATION plus ROUTED-TO-PER-NPC-PHILOSOPHY-DISPATCH).

## Decision 3: Per-NPC-philosophy-profile ablation-gate Condition-2 modification per Substrate D

**Modified Condition 2 hierarchical regression blocks** per Substrate D's specification:
- **Block 0 (controls)**: sim_id plus population_type plus game year plus event family plus actor-and-target role plus dyad prior-history length plus relationship strength plus random intercepts for NPC nested in Sim
- **Block 1 (existing decomposition)**: valence plus arousal plus stress plus sentiment plus attachment plus typed-index category plus HEXACO-H plus other active trait covariates
- **Block 2 (NEW - reduced appraisal baselines)**: `agency_attribution` plus `expectedness_delta` plus `goal_congruence` plus `coping_control_estimate` plus deliberately-lossy `norm_violation_tag` as generic universal-tag baseline
- **Block 3 (profile-specific)**: `normative_profile_assignment` plus `profile_norm_violation_score` plus `profile × event_norm_signature` interaction plus `profile_norm_violation_score × relationship_strength` interaction plus profile-family × population_type interactions

**Pass criteria** per Substrate D's specification (the modified Condition 2 passes when profile dispatch adds unique variance beyond appraisal-tag baseline):
- Pooled Delta-R-squared for Block 2 to Block 3 at minimum 0.05
- Cohen's f-squared at minimum 0.02
- Bootstrap lower confidence bound clears the pre-specified threshold
- Per-population effects are directionally coherent (no population-family reversal without documented reason)
- Cross-validated log loss or Brier score improves for policy choices plus memory salience

**Fail criteria** per Substrate D's specification:
- The generic appraisal-tag block absorbs the same variance profile dispatch would (the generic universal-tag adequately substitutes for profile-family-specific rules)
- Profile assignment only proxies demographics (the profile assignment is empirically-driven by demographic features rather than philosophy-family-specific normative content)
- Profile labels become decorative (labels present but don't drive behavioral divergence)
- The Wiredu-versus-Gyekye distinction vanishes after sentiment plus trait plus structural appraisal controls (the per-philosophy-family normative rule-set specificity does not survive the appraisal-tag-baseline block)
