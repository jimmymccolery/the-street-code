Trovato et al." at five recurring locations (paper lines 168 + 335 + 595 + 733 + 930). Substrate A' extracted the running-header template placeholder as the paper's author attribution. The mechanical failure is at the citation-metadata-extraction surface where Substrate A' honored the wrong text region of the PDF.
